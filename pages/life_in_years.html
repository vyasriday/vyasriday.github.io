2020 is Going Fast

The Idea is Simple

  • Your Past is Gone, There is no way to access it. Don't waste your time crying over it. You can't uncheck your past.

  • Future is Yet To Come, but not necessarily. Don't live too much into it. You can't check in to your future, but you can plan it.

  • Present is what you have access to. Each day spent wisely makes you regret your past less and see your future better.
